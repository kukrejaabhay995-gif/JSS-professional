Affiliation Support & Certification

We provide comprehensive, end-to-end guidance for obtaining and maintaining school affiliations with leading educational boards, including CBSE, ICSE, State Boards, and internationally recognized boards such as Cambridge Assessment International Education and International Baccalaureate.

Our services are designed to simplify the complex affiliation process while ensuring full compliance with all regulatory requirements.

What We Offer

1. Documentation & Application

We assist in preparing and reviewing all necessary documents, including trust/society registration, land certificates, building safety, and NOCs. Our team ensures your application meets board-specific criteria for faster approvals.

2. Infrastructure & Compliance

Guidance on meeting mandatory infrastructure norms such as classroom size, laboratories, libraries, sports facilities, and safety standards. We help align your campus with board regulations from the start.

3. Policy & Academic Framework

We support the development of school policies, curriculum planning, assessment structures, and academic calendars as per the chosen board’s guidelines.

4. Inspection & Audit Prep

Get fully prepared for board inspections with mock audits, checklist validation, and expert recommendations to address gaps before official visits.

5. Staff Recruitment & Training

Advisory on hiring qualified teaching and administrative staff, along with training aligned to board expectations and modern pedagogy.

6. Digital Systems & Records

Implementation of ERP systems, student data management, and compliance tracking tools required by boards.

7. Renewal & Upgradation

Ongoing support for renewal processes, extension of affiliation, or upgrading school levels (e.g., from primary to senior secondary).

Why Choose Us

  • ✓ Deep understanding of multi-board affiliation processes
  • ✓ Faster, error-free application handling
  • ✓ End-to-end support from planning to approval
  • ✓ Continuous compliance and advisory services

What We Offer

1. Integrated Campaign Strategy (ATL / BTL / TTL)

End-to-end planning and execution of Above-the-Line (mass media), Below-the-Line (on-ground), and Through-the-Line (integrated) campaigns for maximum reach and impact.

2. Lead Gen & Funnel Optimization

Creation of high-converting lead funnels—from awareness to inquiry to enrollment—using data-driven strategies and automation tools.

3. Digital Marketing Excellence

Targeted campaigns across search engines, social media, and display platforms to reach the right audience at the right time with compelling creatives.

4. Admissions Campaign Management

Strategic planning of admission cycles, including launch campaigns, open house promotions, and seasonal marketing pushes.

5. Performance Marketing & Analytics

Continuous tracking of campaign performance with KPIs like cost per lead (CPL), conversion rates, and ROI—ensuring optimized spending and better results.

6. Content & Creative Strategy

Development of engaging content—videos, ad creatives, landing pages, and storytelling—that resonates with parents and students.

7. On-ground Activations & Outreach

Execution of events, school fairs, seminars, and local outreach programs to build trust and direct engagement with prospective families.

Why It Works

  • ✓ Ensures consistent brand presence across all channels
  • ✓ Generates high-intent, quality leads
  • ✓ Improves conversion rates and reduces acquisition costs
  • ✓ Aligns marketing efforts directly with admissions goals

Sales & CRM Enablement

We empower your admissions team with the right strategies, tools, and processes to convert inquiries into enrollments efficiently. By combining structured sales funnels, advanced CRM systems, and parent engagement frameworks, we ensure a seamless and high-conversion admissions journey.

What We Offer

1. Admissions Funnel Design & Optimization

We design a clear, data-driven admissions funnel—from inquiry to enrollment—identifying drop-off points and optimizing each stage for higher conversions.

2. CRM Selection & Deployment

Implementation of modern CRM systems tailored for schools, enabling centralized lead management, automated follow-ups, and real-time tracking of admissions data.

3. Sales Team Training & Playbooks

Comprehensive training programs for admissions counselors, including scripts, objection handling, follow-up strategies, and conversion techniques.

4. Lead Management & Automation

Streamlining lead capture, nurturing, and segmentation through automation workflows—ensuring no inquiry is missed or delayed.

5. Parent Engagement Frameworks

Structured communication strategies including personalized emails, WhatsApp updates, campus visit experiences, and post-inquiry engagement to build trust.

6. Conversion Tracking & Reporting

Setup of dashboards and reporting systems to monitor key metrics such as inquiry-to-visit ratio, visit-to-admission rate, and counselor performance.

7. Retention & Relationship Management

Ongoing engagement strategies to strengthen relationships with enrolled families, encouraging referrals and long-term loyalty.

Why It Matters

  • ✓ Improves inquiry-to-admission conversion rates
  • ✓ Reduces response time and lead leakage
  • ✓ Enhances parent experience and trust
  • ✓ Brings transparency and accountability to admissions teams
